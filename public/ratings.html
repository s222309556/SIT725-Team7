Points System

Earning Points:

With each book exchange, you earn 5 points. The more you share, the more you gain!

Tiers and Benefits:

The more points you accumulate, the higher your tier. Starting from Bronze (10 points), to Silver (20 points), and Gold (30 points), each tier comes with its unique perks.

Redeeming Points:

You can redeem your points for free books and vouchers. Every 100 points get you a free book, and every 50 books exchanged get you a voucher!
